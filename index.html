Session 2 / Healing
What Are You Carrying?
Healing Unresolved Emotions, Wounds, and Patterns
You cannot build freely while carrying what God is asking you to surrender. This session names the baggage, wounds, and patterns that follow people into every relationship.
01
Baggage Check
What unresolved baggage have you been carrying?
  • Anger from something in my family
  • Still feel rejected by someone important
  • Compare myself to someone often
  • Feel overlooked or unnoticed
  • Replay old conversations or pain
  • Carry insecurity I rarely talk about
✓ Sent
02
Wound Check
What wounds have affected you or still affect you?
  • Rejection
  • Abandonment
  • Betrayal
  • Favoritism
  • Family conflict
  • Shame
  • Church hurt
  • Fear / Insecurity
✓ Sent
03
Pattern Check
What patterns have you noticed in your life?
  • I avoid hard conversations.
  • I get angry quickly.
  • I assume people will leave.
  • I repeat patterns from my home.
  • I keep going back to what hurts me.
✓ Sent
04
Final Reflection
What are you carrying that God is asking you to surrender?
